Beginning

The Jardin Royal restaurant was founded in the late 1980's. The restaurant is situated in Newbridge, Kildare. The restaurant was the first Chinese restaurant to arrive in Kildare. In the beginning, the restaurant became very popular with the locals.

Question

Ownership

The Jardin Royal is owned by the Hui family. The original founder of the restaurant was Jimmy Hui. In the early 1990's, Jimmy decided to retire and give the ownership of his restaurant to his son, Kam Hui.

Question

Today

Today, The Jardin Royal has massively improved its services and food sice then. Because of the new management, The Jardin Royal is now the best Chinese restaurants in the world.
